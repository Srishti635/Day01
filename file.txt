Q1 What is data
Data refers to raw, unorganized facts and figures that can be processed and analyzed to extract meaningful information. It can exist in various forms, including numbers, text, images, and sound
 Essentially, data is the foundation upon which information and knowledge are built
Q2 What is filesystem
A filesystem is a method used by operating systems to organize, store, and manage files and directories on a storage device. It acts like a digital file cabinet, structuring how data is stored and allowing users and applications to efficiently access and retrieve information.
